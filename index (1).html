Agarwood (Oud) Specimen

The Liquid Gold of Perfumery

Agarwood trees are universally renowned for producing *Oud*—the world's most fragrant, dense, and precious resin. Due to dwindling natural supplies and soaring global demand across luxury haute perfumery, traditional medicine, and ceremonial incense, sustainably farmed Agarwood commands exceptional valuations.
